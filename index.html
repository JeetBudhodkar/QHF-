Rules & Guidelines
Mandatory for all attending college groups
📜
Compliance is mandatory. Non-compliance may result in restricted entry.
1
Reporting Time & Entry
  • Report strictly within your allotted time slot.
  • Late arrivals registered only after 30 min delay, subject to crowd conditions.
  • Entry through designated gates only. Re-entry not permitted without organizer approval.
2
Registration
  • Mandatory for all members. Follow the queue and cooperate with registration staff.
  • Two steps: signature/attendance verification, then ID card or entry pass collection.
3
Identity & Access
  • Wear issued ID card at all times. Entry without College ID + Batch is not allowed.
  • IDs are non-transferable.
4
Luggage
  • Large bags, backpacks and luggage strictly prohibited.
  • Only small handbags or essential pouches allowed. Organizers not liable for lost items.
5
Vehicles & Parking
  • Share vehicle number in advance when contacted by the committee.
  • Only pre-informed vehicles get parking access. Follow on-site parking instructions.
